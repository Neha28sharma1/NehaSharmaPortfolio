This is an assignment for the course Content design for New Media, where we were tasked with creating pieces for our portfolio. This is Portfolio Assignment #3 - Magazine Cover. I have designed a magazine cover for fashion magazine Glam . The typography’s black, white, and red color palette embodies the magazine's bold, elegant, and timeless style. Together, these colors create a striking contrast that mirrors the magazine's dual focus on cutting-edge trends and timeless elegance. This palette ensures a high-fashion editorial feel, appealing to both contemporary and classic sensibilities. The combination of Anton and Courage typface reflects the magazine’s mission to blend bold innovation with classic elegance. The magazine’s style can be described as chic, stylish, and modern.

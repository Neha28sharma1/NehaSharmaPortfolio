This is an assignment for the course Content design for New Media, where we were tasked with creating pieces for our portfolio. This is Portfolio Assignment #1 - Design Your Own Brand. I have designed a logo for Pixel Forge, a company specializing in digital solutions like web design and development. To make the design personal, I combined the letters "P" and "F" in my own handwriting using a pen tablet. The bright colors I chose reflect the brand's creativity and vibrancy, visually capturing its innovative and dynamic approach.

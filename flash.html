Pop-Up Art Gallery

Illustrator/Photoshop/After Effects

This assignment is part of the course Content Design for New Media, where we were tasked with developing content for a pop-up art gallery. The challenge was to create a toolbox that visually represents and identifies the gallery’s unique profile. The design should be tailored to meet the needs and expectations of a modern company, with a strong emphasis on digital elements and online engagement. The concept should not only establish a cohesive visual identity but also translate seamlessly into social media platforms, ensuring the gallery’s presence resonates with contemporary audiences. The foundational idea was to flash people with art, calling their feelings of nostalgia and surprise.
